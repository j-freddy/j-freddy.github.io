## Philosophy

<!--
  - vision: create a site that presents and maintains my identity
    - this is reflected in the design / style of the site
    - also in some easter eggs
  - side vision: to write and maintain (mostly tech-related) articles
    - for myself: to have chance to present research on topics of my owninterest
      and topics beyond my university curriculum, and to practice writing blog
      posts
    - for the community: to provide interesting, accessible and
      beginner-friendly[1] content, where the reader can be fully immersed and
      not be victim to artificial distractions (e.g. advertisments, pop-ups, and
      more minor distractions)
      - do not span the entire horizontal viewport (it has a similar width to
        fit 80 characters, which is originally from the 80-char old fasioned
        typewriters but is actually good for reading on screen)
  
  [1]for the most part, and at least for the starting sections of the article
-->

## Frameworks, Tools and APIs

<!--
  - Jekyll
    - theme: Minima is the default theme, do not use a pre-build theme so I can
      fully customise my site as my vision is to create a site that presents my
      identity
  - BootStrap
    - because vanilla HTML isn't industrial practice anymore, for the most part
      using utilities (e.g. margin/padding utilities) and eases creating a
      dynamic site (works for phone & wide screen), restyle built-in components
      like pills
  - SCSS
    - to help organise styling
  - Colour
    - https://yeun.github.io/open-color/
  - FontAwesome
-->
